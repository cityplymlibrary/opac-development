How long can I borrow items from the Digital Village for?

To keep things simple, we've recently introduced the 'Easy as 1, 2, 3' rule for borrowing books, equipment and computers.

  1. Items such as short-term Laptop loans, tablets, etc are available for 1-day loans.
  2. Items that might be necessary to keep overnight such as cameras and camera accessories i.e. tripods are available for 2-day loans.
  3. Other items that are necessary for longer such as books, accessibility hardware, physical journals and, long-term laptop loans are available for 3-week loans.

It is worth noting that digital resources such as databases, eBooks and eJournals are usually made available for instant access on-demand. Please check the catalogue per-item for more information.

Help! I've forgotten my College password! How can I get access to my College account again?

Before coming to see us, make sure you're using the correct username for the service you want to access. Systems such as Moodle, bksb and Shibboleth (inc the Catalogue site) will expect only your student ID number (e.g. 10712345). To access Google services, you will need to put cityplym.ac.uk after your student ID number (e.g. 10712345@cityplym.ac.uk).

Provided you're confident you're using the correct username, or alltogether can't remember your password, you now have two options:

 

  1. If you are able to come on-site, the fastest method of getting a new password is by visiting the main counter at the Digital Village. Refer to FAQ #1 for instructions on how to find us. Please remember to bring photo ID - preferably an up-to-date student ID card, otherwise a passport or driver's licence so we can issue you with a College ID card (Â£1 charge for a replacement).
  2. If you are unable to come on-site, you will need to complete the IT Support request form and wait five working days for a password reset. Within this five working day window, you will receive a telephone call from the College so please be prepared to answer calls from unknown numbers.

 

For more information on password resets, please visit Student Central and look for the 'Password Reset' link.

What academic support can I get from the Digital Village?

The Digital Village is home to both the Tutor Librarian team, and the IT Support technicians. Between these two teams, you should find all the support you need to excel in your studies, both in-person and throughout your online studies.

  • Study Skills sessions, delivered by the Tutor Librarians, are by-appointment one-to-one sessions which can be arranged to help you become a better learner. This can be non-topical support on how to write a good essay, how to reference, the best resources and information sources for your essays, or grammar and literary support.
  • IT Support sessions, delivered by the IT Support technicians, are by-appointment on-to-one sessions which can be arranged to give you the confidence you need to improve your IT and Google skills. From Docs to Sheets, Classroom to Koha, we can help through tailored tutoring sessions on whatever IT-related topic you need us to go over.

If you would like more information on these sessions or would like to book, please click on the button below to get started!
