Where is the Digital Village?

The Digital Village is located on the first floor of the link building, joining the STEM Centre, Inovation & refectory, and tower block buildings.

The space can be accessed either from the first floor of the STEM Centre by passing through the Maths Centre of Excellence (follow the signs from Reception) or on the first floor of the tower block near the double lifts (again, follow signs from the lifts).

What types of equipment are available through the Digital Village?

The Digital Village plays host to the College's traditional Library services as well as a comprehensive collection of online resources, eBooks and physical digital equipment such as video cameras and loanable laptops.

How long can I borrow items from the Digital Village for?

To keep things simple, we've recently introduced the 'Easy as 1, 2, 3' rule for borrowing books, equipment and computers.

  1. Items such as short-term Laptop loans, tablets, etc are available for 1-day loans.
  2. Items that might be necessary to keep overnight such as cameras and camera accessories i.e. tripods are available for 2-day loans.
  3. Other items that are necessary for longer such as books, accessibility hardware, physical journals and, long-term laptop loans are available for 3-week loans.

It is worth noting that digital resources such as databases, eBooks and eJournals are usually made available for instant access on-demand. Please check the catalogue per-item for more information.

Help! I've forgotten my College password! How can I get access to my College account again?

Before coming to see us, make sure you're using the correct username for the service you want to access. Systems such as Moodle, bksb and Shibboleth (inc the Catalogue site) will expect only your student ID number (e.g. 10712345). To access Google services, you will need to put cityplym.ac.uk after your student ID number (e.g. 10712345@cityplym.ac.uk).

Provided you're confident you're using the correct username, or alltogether can't remember your password, you now have two options:

  1. If you are able to come on-site, the fastest method of getting a new password is by visiting the main counter at the Digital Village. Refer to FAQ #1 for instructions on how to find us.
