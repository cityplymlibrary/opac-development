The Digital Village

The Digital Village encompases our Library and self-directed study spaces, as well as our events and lecturing theatre named 'The Apple'. We're found on the first floor of the link building, accessed near the lifts on the first floor of the tower block.

If you're interested and want to learn more, click the button below to visit our Digital Village page - where you'll also find information about up-and-coming events and open-access lectures.
