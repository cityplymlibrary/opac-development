Get to know the team

The Digital Village team exists cross-College and includes a diverse and multi-talented set of individuals. Whatever your academic, research, or Library needs, someone in our department will be able to help. If you'd like to know us better, why not pop in? You can find information on how to find us on the Our Space page.
